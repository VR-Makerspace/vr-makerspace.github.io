About Us

Established in AY 2019/20 Semester 2, VRNM is a student-led Virtual Reality (VR) makerspace project under the module NM4210 User Experience Design.

The team behind VRNM designed and simulated a makerspace that centres on virtual reality, where makers will be inspired by the potential of VR and equipped with supportive resources to create VR projects.

Welcome to VRNM - our idea of what a VR makerspace would look like, that fosters immersive learning and inspires innovation among Communication and New Media (CNM) students!

Our Journey

25 CNM students embarked on a 13-week journey of research, user-testing, content creation, and execution, in an effort to simulate a conducive virtual reality makerspace.

Vision

To be the pioneering Virtual Reality Makerspace in Singapore, where makers embrace immersive learning and inspire innovation.

Mission

We provide a conducive communal space for makers to learn independently and experiment collaboratively through virtual reality.

What To Expect

We offer a suite of equipment, furniture and tools to empower you as you learn more about VR and develop your own VR projects.

Making Corner

A 2m by 2m space functioning as a playtest area where a group of up to 4 people can work collaboratively and creatively together.

Making Booth

A 1m by 1m space functioning as an individual workstation to encourage focused work that allows for up to 2 people, supporting experiential and creative learning.

Safety

We strive to create a VR makerspace that is both safe and enjoyable for everyone.
Please take note of the following safety hazards that may arise during your use of the makerspace.

Electric shock
Dizziness
Nausea
Trip hazard
Fatigue

Safety Precautions

All members must ensure that others remain safe in the Makerspace at all times.
If you see a member doing something unsafe, alert and assist them.
If you see a potential safety hazard, alert Makerspace staff and members who are in the immediate vicinity.
Members must respect the safety of other members while in the Makerspace, as well as their own safety.

Our Projects

Making a Virtual Garden

Users will learn how to create their own virtual garden which they can explore with a VR headset in this project, and combine their skills with their imagination to create other virtual environments of their own.

Creating an Interactive Story

In this project, users will learn how to create an interactive story of Little Red Riding Hood that can be viewed on VR, complete with nonplayer characters and conditional dialogues.

Making an Interactive Piano

Users will learn how to create an interactive piano that can be played in VR, and then turn it into a fun musical puzzle. They will also pick up basic JavaScript skills that they can apply to make their own games.

Rules and Regulations

  1. Only students, staff and authorized personnel of the National University of Singapore are allowed to use the VR Makerspace.
  2. The use of the VR Makerspace will be strictly for users who have booked the system. Books/bags used to reserve places will be confiscated.
  3. All users are required to show their matriculation/staff identity cards upon request. You will be asked to leave the facility if you are an unauthorised user.
  4. The use of the VR Makerspace is solely for academic purposes – it is not to be used for commercial work.
  5. Smoking, drinking or eating is not allowed in the VR Makerspace.
  6. Keep the place clean and organized, be considerate to all users at all times.
  7. Users are not permitted to block fire exits or safety and first aid equipment.
  8. Vandalism, theft and other abuses of these facilities are serious disciplinary offences that will be reported to the University for further action.
  9. Do not leave personal items unattended in the VR Makerspace.
  10. The University is not responsible for any loss of data or loss of personal belongings in the facility.
