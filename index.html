About Us

We are Singapore's first VR makerspace, started by a group of students from the Department of Communications and New Media (CNM) in the National University of Singapore. Our goal is to make virtual reality accessible to all and empower students across CNM to get started with the technology easily and seamlessly.

Our makerspace provides a suite of projects and tutorials which introduce visitors to the fundamentals of creating compelling and immersive experiences in virtual reality.
